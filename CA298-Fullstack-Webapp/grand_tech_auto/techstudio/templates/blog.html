GlNine Smart Monitor Stand


Say goodbye to hunching and slouching! GINine’s Smart monitor stands might be everything you’re looking for to enhance your home office aesthetics and maximise your desk usage space. These smart monitor stands have one purpose in mind: to offer its user with a clean work environment. These new an improved stands feature a 15 degree raise making it the perfect height for your eye level to relieve that neck and shoulder pain. Use the stand to provide a healthy and comfortable position to help you with your posture whilst you work from home or in the office.

These stands are made of high quality aluminum alloy and a professional reinforcement structure that can support up to 66 pounds and a monitor up to 40”. The stands are of ergonomic design and come with 4 USB 3.0 ports. These smart monitor stands are portable and very easy to assemble.

GINine's Smart monitor stand has universal compatibility and boasts universal use. It can work with all laptops up to 40" inches, such as: Chromebook, HP, Lenovo, Macbook, Macbook Air, Macbook Pro, Microsoft Surface, Google Pixelbook, Sony, Dell, Acer, ASUS and more. It also makes for a great gift and would work well as a Christmas, Secret Santa, Birthday or thoughtful gift for any occasion.

In our opinion GINine’s Smart Monitor Stand is well worth the money. You may think it may be a little expensive for a stand but it is far from just a stand, with its excellent build and design.
